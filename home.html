Our Company : In a Box

We source your ingredients directly from the producer and deliver to your home without any detours.
Your order box comes with pre-measured ingredients which helps to avoid food waste.
This shorter supply chain means we avoid packaging and repackaging along the way.
As a result, your order foods from our company create 25%* fewer carbon emissions than the same meals
made from supermarket ingredients.

Sustainability Report 2021

2021 marked In a Box's 5-year anniversary of celebrating innovation in the food industry and
our commitment to minimising our impact on the environment. Take a look at our 2021
Sustainability Report and see how we analysed our climate impact through a comprehensive
life cycle assessment. Understanding our carbon footprint across the value chain empowers
our customers to make more sustainable food choices.

Keeping More on Your Plate

When food waste goes to landfill, it impacts the climate by producing greenhouse
gases. In total, many households waste 4.5 million tonnes of food every year1.
We help you reduce food waste by purchasing ingredients based on your order,
pre-portioned to the gram.

No Detours

We order all ingredients directly from our suppliers, so your groceries are delivered straight
to your home without stopping at warehouses and retailers. This saves CO₂ emissions by
cutting back on transport time and extra storage. We've also committed to offsetting
100% of our direct CO2 emissions, making us the most systematic and following
environment rules company.

Innovative Packaging

Your ingredients are always of the highest quality and are delivered to your door
fresh and safe. We are continuously innovating to find more
sustainable options or to remove packaging altogether.
